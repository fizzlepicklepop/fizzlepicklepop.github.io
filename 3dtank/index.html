Tiny Tanks RTC

A static 3D browser tank duel inspired by Wii Play's tank minigame. Host on GitHub Pages, share WebRTC strings, and play peer-to-peer.

Multiplayer flow: player 1 clicks Host and sends the string. Player 2 pastes it and clicks Join, then sends the answer back. Player 1 pastes the answer and clicks Accept Answer.
Controls: Keyboard W/S drive, A/D turn, Space fire, Shift brake. Controller: left stick drive/turn, A / R2 fire. Ricochet shells, hide behind blocks, first to 5 wins.
